We are happy to present TrainServ, which integrates multiple sources of data and converts into easy-to-use information to help ops and planning do “more” for “less”:

  • “More”: Improve performance, timetables and safety for passengers and freight
  • “Less”: Save staff time and traction energy
Partnering with LNER, GWR, Crossrail, Network Rail , London Overground and GTR we are helping the industry generate value to build back better through data and the latest tech.

LJMU in partnership with Merseyrail presents IRIS.

IRIS is a real-time information and communication system designed to meet the needs of rail operations. From giving control room staff the insight they need to minimise the impact of delays to enabling the interstation comms for smoother assisted travel, IRIS puts the information staff need at their fingertips. This real-time provision of information will help staff to react and make decisions instantly to improve passenger experience.

LJMU in partnership with Merseyrail presents IRIS. IRIS is a real-time information and communication system designed to meet the needs of rail operations. From giving control room staff the insight they need to minimise the impact of delays to enabling the interstation comms for smoother assisted travel, IRIS puts the information staff need at their fingertips. This real-time provision of information will help staff to react and make decisions instantly to improve passenger experience.

Putting Passengers First

March 17th 2021

A train is delayed.

How the operator responds in the next few minutes, few days and overtime will impact

  • - how many passengers are affected and how badly,
  • - how those who are affected feel about the experience, and
  • - how often similar delays reoccur in the future.

Join this event to discover how three digital projects help operators address this and other scenarios to improve passenger experience and rebuild confidence in rail as the mode of choice.

Cogitare, Liverpool John Moores University and Esoterix Systems are excited to present these outcomes as part of the SBRI First of a Kind in Rail 2020 competition, funded by the Department for Transport and delivered by Innovate UK.

Rail Innovation Exhibition

9th - 12th February 2021

This event will be a showcase of the best in rail innovation brought to you by Innovate UK, KTN, the Department for Transport (DfT), and the Railway Industry Association (RIA), in partnership with UKRRIN and Network Rail. It will bring together a fantastic line-up to showcase some of the most exciting innovations and projects the rail sector has to offer.

Attendees at the virtual event will be able to visit virtual exhibition stands, hear presentations ranging from customer experience and accessibility to sustainable stations and rolling stock, and participate in conference themes. Across the four days, there will be sessions highlighting some of the winners of the DfT-funded ‘First of a Kind’ Rail Demonstration and Accelerating Innovation in Rail competitions, as well as major announcements.
